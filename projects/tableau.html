Dashboard Features

  • Interactive filters for date range, channel, and campaign, allowing drill-down from top-level totals to individual campaign performance.
  • KPI summary cards showing total Spend, Impressions, Clicks, Conversions, CTR, CPC, CPA, and ROAS at a glance.
  • Trend line chart tracking daily and monthly spend and conversions over 2 years, with clear Q4 seasonality peaks visible.
  • Channel comparison bar charts showing how Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, and TikTok stack up on efficiency metrics (CPC, CPA, ROAS).
  • Campaign-level performance table ranking all 12 campaigns by ROAS, enabling quick identification of top and underperforming campaigns.
